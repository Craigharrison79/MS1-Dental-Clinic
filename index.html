Our Services

Public Dental Care

Taking care of your oral health is just as important as taking care of your health in general. Therefore, it is very important to have your teeth examined regularly once a year in order to be able to detect and prevent damage at an early stage, which, if you let it go too far, can become significantly more extensive and thus more costly than necessary.

Prosthetics & Implants

Prosthetics means that lost or severely damaged teeth are replaced with crowns and bridges, implants or dentures. If you are missing one or more teeth, an implant is what most resembles a "regular" tooth. With us, we have the opportunity, in collaboration with our surgeon, to make reimbursements of this type. Your implant is attached with a titanium screw that forms a new "root" and to which we then attach a crown. A dental implant lasts a lifetime, just like a healthy tooth, provided that good oral hygiene is maintained so that inflammation is kept away.

Aesthetic Dentistry

We can help you repair broken teeth, replace missing teeth or bleached teeth that have discolored. We work with well-proven materials to ensure an aesthetic and sustainable result.

Replacement of amalgam fillings

By replacing amalgam with other materials, such as composite or ceramic materials, you get whiter teeth that are more aesthetically pleasing. The treatment is usually as simple as repairing a regular hole. We drill away the amalgam and replace it with composite. You can use your teeth immediately after the treatment.

About

Linda Hagstam Harrison

Linda started her carrier in dentistry over 12 years ago as a dental hygienist. Sine then she has studied furthr to become a dentist and graduated in 2016 from Malmö University, Sweden. Since graduation she's been working in private dentistry as family dentist doing most procedures in general dentistry. She has developed a special interest in cosmetic dentistry and she is an Invisalign provider and a member of the Swedish Academy for Cosmetic Dentistry.

New Patient

Linda believes in keeping teeth natural and healthy for as long as possible and are working closely with her patients to prevent the most common dental diseases; caries and peroidontal disease. She has also got a deep understanding of the power of beautiful smiles and smile design and has helped many patients to improve their self-esteem simply by changing the aesthetices of their teeth. She uses facially-driven smile design and is working with highly skilled dental technicians both within Sweden as well as abroad to create the best possible outcomes for her patients.
